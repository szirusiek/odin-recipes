Scrambled eggs

A breakfast staple upgraded to its master form - scrambled eggs with cheese! Perfectly salty, delightfully creamy and, well, 100% egg-y!

Ingredients

  • 3 large eggs
  • 2 triangles of Zott cheese
  • Butter
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. In a bowl, lightly scramble 3 eggs with a whisk
  2. Add the Zott cheese, salt and pepper to the eggs
  3. Continue whisking until the cheese breaks into pea-size pieces
  4. On a pan, melt 3 tablespoons of butter
  5. Change the heat to small and pour in the eggs
  6. Stir constantly with a whisk, until the mixture thickens
  7. When desired consistency is achieved, place the dish onto a plate

Bon Appetit! With this breakfast, your body will experience a real taste and energy kick right at the beginning of your day!
