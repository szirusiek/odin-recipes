Spaghetti Carbonara

Simple, yet tasteful and, most importantly, quick and cheap recipe for everyone. You need next to zero skills to make this meal, but you'll feel like a 5-star chef after the first bite!

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti pasta
  • Heavy cream (30% or 36%)
  • Bacon
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Bring water to boil in a big pot and salt it
  2. Put the pasta in the water
  3. Cut the bacon into stripes and put on a cold pan, frying it on low setting
  4. While pasta is cooking, combine heavy cream, cheese, salt and pepper
  5. Sieve the pasta, don't wash it and let the bacon fry until slightly golden
  6. Toss the pasta onto the pan with the bacon, coat everything with the grease
  7. Add the cheese-cream mixture into the pasta, stirring everything constantly
  8. Once everything combined and thickened, plate the dish and sprinkle some shredded cheese on top and garnish to your liking
